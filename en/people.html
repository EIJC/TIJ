Tarek Khello

Tarek Khello is a Syrian journalist, who worked for several local media in Aleppo until the end of 2012. Due to his work as journalist he had been threatened by officials. In 2012 he escaped to Beirut, Lebanon, where he was accepted onto the special refugees resettlement programme thanks to the intervention of the United Nations High Commission for Refugees.
