Dr. Lutz Mükke

Dr. Lutz Mükke is the Academic Director of the European Institute for Journalism and Communication Research (EIJC) since March 2014 and Project Manager of “International Journalist´s Meetings”. Besides he is the Project Manager of the newly established European Centre for Press and Media Freedom. Lutz Mükke worked for print media and TV in conflict and war zones like DR Congo, Somalia, Afghanistan and Southern Sudan.

Tarek Khello

Tarek Khello is a Syrian journalist, who worked for several local media in Aleppo until the end of 2012. Due to his work as journalist he had been threatened by officials. In 2012 he escaped to Beirut, Lebanon, where he was accepted onto the special refugees resettlement programme thanks to the intervention of the United Nations High Commission for Refugees. Since December 2013 he has lived in Germany. In Leipzig he works as a volunteer for several aid organisations focused on the integration of refugees. He supports the project International Journalist´s Meetings in aspects of research and public relations. Tarek Khello workes as freelancer and is part of Mediendienst Ost.

Annegret Ode

Annegret Ode, M.A., worked until 2015 as research assistant at EIJC. She continues to organize the International Journalist´s Meetings as her volunteer project.
